Ingredients Of Fafda
2 cups besan (chick-pea flour)
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp ajwain (thymol)
1/4 tsp haldi (turmeric)
1 Tbsp oil
1/2 tsp salt tsp or to taste
Oil to deep-fry
How to Make Fafda
1.Mix the besan, soda, ajwain, haldi, 1 tbsp oil and salt together.2.Add water and knead into a soft dough.3.Take a portion of the dough and place it on a greased surface.4.With base of your palm, drag the dough in a straight line, holding the dough at the starting point with the other hand.5.This will form a strip.6.Fry these strips over medium heat till crisp.
